Biography

Hello! I am Dr. Md. Muin Uddin, a researcher and educator specializing in renewable energy technologies, thin-film PV systems, and hydrogen fuel cells. My academic journey started with a Ph.D. from the University of Science and Technology of China, where I conducted groundbreaking research on energy-efficient building technologies. Currently, I am an Assistant Professor at Primeasia University, sharing my expertise with future engineers and researchers.
