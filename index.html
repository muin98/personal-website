Biography

Hello! I am Dr. Md. Muin Uddin, a researcher and educator specializing in renewable energy technologies and photovoltaic systems. With a Ph.D. from the University of Science and Technology of China, my work focuses on energy efficiency and sustainable building solutions.

Research Interests

My research interests include:

  • Photovoltaic applications in buildings
  • Energy efficiency and solar energy utilization
  • Thin-film PV cell technologies
  • Hydrogen production and fuel cells
