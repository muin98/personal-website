Biography

Hello! I am Dr. Md. Muin Uddin, a researcher and educator specializing in renewable energy technologies and photovoltaic systems. With a Ph.D. from the University of Science and Technology of China, my work focuses on energy efficiency and sustainable building solutions. I have a passion for contributing to sustainable development through innovative research and effective teaching methodologies.

As an Assistant Professor at Primeasia University, I am dedicated to inspiring and empowering the next generation of engineers. My academic journey and professional experience in renewable energy have shaped my commitment to excellence in research and education.
